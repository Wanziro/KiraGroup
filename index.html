About KiraGroup

KiraGroup is a non-governmantal organization working in Rwanda since 2019. Our vision is to inspire and support youth and women to build the future they want. We aim to do this by providing short term educational trainings, workshops, capacity buildings that empowers them to have access to the decent jobs and enterprenuership opportunities. In 2018, Igire Rwanda partned with US Embassy Kigali to launch SheCanCODE high quality coding bootcamp for young women in Rwanda aiming to prepare and help young women to start new career as software developers and reduce inqualities in tech industry.

Mission

The mission of Igire Rwanda Organization is to assist women and youth to amass knowledge and skills that lead to their self-reliance and enable them to create opportunities for other women and youth.

Vision

To target and work with women and youth in their communities across the country to empower them to create their own individual, dynamic life plans and supporting them in achieving their goals through the improving access to knowledge, information and basic skills.
