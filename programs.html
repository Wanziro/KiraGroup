Kira Education

Education is and will continue to be a key to success. Individuals attend schools for their employability and other opportunities. In Rwanda, individuals have access to 9 – 12 Years Basic Education which is a good starting point. However, both high school and university graduates lack employment because they lack the essential skills, they need to be employable or start their small businesses. Kira Group has educational programs that help graduates to acquire skills they need to qualify for employment and be ready to start their small enterprises.

Kira Rubyiruko

Rwanda graduates approximately 10,000 – 12,000 in secondary school and 8,000 – 10,000 in universities every year. The majority of high school graduates lack access to tertiary education because they do not qualify, they are unable to afford the cost of university courses or other reasons. University graduates start looking for job opportunities that they might not be qualified for. Kira Rubyiruko focuses on training them on the key skills they need to start their own small businesses that would create jobs for their colleagues.

Kira Rubyiruko is a 3 months intensive entrepreneurial program that focusing on helping graduates (both high school and university) acquire entrepreneurial skills they need to start or grow their own businesses. We focus on the basic business concepts, design thinking, business ideation and planning, legal aspects of a business, business marketing, public speaking and other important skills. Our program participants get a chance to interact with successful individuals in business and they get a chance to ask questions or get mentored. At the end of the program, each program participant should have an implementable business plan, ability to compete for funds, RDB Business Registration Certificate, have online presence and other tools that smoothen their business journey.

Our successful program participants should be able to:

  • Create an implementable business plan
  • Create and participate in a business pitch competition
  • Register their businesses with RBD
  • Have online presence for their businesses (Instagram, Twitter and / or Website)
  • Etc

Kira Tech

It is arguable that mastering the use of technology is one of the most important skills job seekers and / or entrepreneurs should master. The ability to write professional documents (Microsoft Words), make good and successful presentations (PowerPoint), analyze the information (Excel), attend meetings (Zoom, Hangout, GMeet, Skype….) and other important skills. These skills are not only helpful while applying and performing job opportunities but also while performing job responsibilities. Kira group also helps participants with job application information and may help in the application process.

Kira Professional

We offer professional competency sessions that help our program participants acquire the professional skills they need to become employable and good employees. These include communication, problem solving, time management, customer relationship management, conflict resolution and other important skills. Mastering these skills will increase job seekers’ competency and employability and will help the employed be more productive in what they do.

Kira Health

We work with individuals and organizations to fight different diseases that defeat Rwandans. We are currently working with the Confederation of Meningitis Organizations to defeat Meningitis. We also fight Malaria, SIDA and other NCDs.

Kira Muhinzi

We help farmers learn the best ways to improve their farming activities and production. We are convinced that farmers take a big part of Rwanda’s life, and their empowerment should be prioritized in all the areas.

We empower farmers to understand the farming practices and other techniques that would improve their harvests.
