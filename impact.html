500 of students SheCanCODE plan to serve by 2023

SheCanCODE graduates enter to the workforce as effective proffionals and ready to become software developers. Many of them are hired after weeks of graduations. During the program, students are introduced on career preparation courses such as resume writing, professional email writing, communication and how to use social networks like Linkedin to search for employment. This prepares them with professional skills and the confidence needed to build their future careers.

The impact on securing the employment is beyong the sucess of the program. Many students who joins our program use this knowledge and skills set to provie for their families due to exposure to a high rising job market in Rwana... Our graduates volunteer in other organization programs which promotes STEM courses in high schools and encouraging other girls to join STEM courses.
